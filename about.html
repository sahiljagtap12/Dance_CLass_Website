Creating Our World

Founded in 2017, ChoRhythm is the brainchild of Director & Founder Sahil Jagtap and Nirajan Veer, along with a fantastic team of dancers who are now a part of the professional company at ChoRhythm. The vision has always been to push boundaries in terms of movement and ideas. Our online dance lessons are as intense and effective as in a real studio. The most difficult aspect is connecting to you, however after you contact us, we can handle everything. You can look forward to the advice of our instructors, dancing in all styles, as well as a lot of fun.
